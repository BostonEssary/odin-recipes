How to make PBJ

Description

Here we are going to make a delicious PBJ that is good for any time of day at any age.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly or Jam of your preferred flavor
  • Your choice of Bread

Instructions

  1. Toast Bread (optional)
  2. Put PB on one slice of the bread
  3. Put jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Connect the bread slices with one another
  5. Profit
