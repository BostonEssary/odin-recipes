How to make Cup Noodles

Description

In this article we are going to discuss how to make everyones favorite budget meal, Cup Noodles.

Ingredients

  • Cup Noodles
  • Water
  • Pot to boil water

Instructions

  1. Put water in to pot
  2. Boil Water
  3. Open Cup Noodles
  4. Put boilling water into Cup Noodles
  5. Cover and Wait 3 mins
  6. Profit
